FAQ's

What is included in the total price?

The value of the trip includes plane tickets from Billund to the destination and back; the tickets from your city of origin to Billund are not included. Every trip includes travel insurance.

If I don't live in Billund, can I still travel with you?

Yes, you can! You have to pay for the ticket from your city to Billund.

I don't speak the language, can I still travel?

Of course you can; we don't speak most of the languages either, but in a group we make ourselves understood and we enjoy the same.

Does the group meet before departure?

We meet at the airport 4/5 hours before the flight so we can meet each other a little bit before.

How do I reserve my place?

You have to go to the "trips" section, select the destination and complete the form with your data. Then, we send you an e-mail with the payment link. Once paid, you have your place!

How many people are we on trips?

We always are 15 people and us.
